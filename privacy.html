Privacy Policy for DesiBhasha

Effective Date: January 01, 2026

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

DesiBhasha ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application operated by Zumily LLC, based in the United States.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app.

DesiBhasha is an educational app designed to help children of Non-Resident Indian (NRI) families learn Indian languages including Hindi, Punjabi, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada, Bangla, and Odiya.

1. Information We Collect

We follow data minimization principles and only collect what is necessary.

a. Personal Information (Parents/Guardians)

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account login details

b. Children’s Information

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without verified parental consent.
  • We may collect:
    • Learning progress (lessons completed, scores)
    • Language preferences

c. Usage Data

  • Features used and interactions
  • Session duration
  • App performance data

d. Device & Technical Data

  • Device type and OS version
  • IP address (may be anonymized)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Personalize learning experiences
  • Track educational progress
  • Improve content and features
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service-related communications

We do NOT use personal data for behavioral advertising targeted at children.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or similar regions, we process data based on:

  • Consent (e.g., account creation, child data)
  • Contractual necessity (to provide app services)
  • Legitimate interests (app improvement, security)
  • Legal obligations

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & App Store Compliance)

DesiBhasha is designed for children under parental supervision. We comply with:

  • COPPA (U.S.)
  • Apple App Store Kids Category Guidelines
  • Google Play Families Policy

Our commitments:

  • No collection of personal data from children without parental consent
  • No behavioral advertising to children
  • No unnecessary data collection
  • Parents can:
    • Review their child’s data
    • Request deletion
    • Withdraw consent

To make such requests, contact: support@desibhasha.com

5. Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell personal data.

We may share data only with:

a. Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics (e.g., Firebase – privacy-friendly settings enabled)
  • Customer support tools

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

b. Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required to:

  • Comply with laws
  • Protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services
  • Comply with legal obligations

You may request deletion anytime at: support@desibhasha.com

7. Data Security

We implement:

  • Encryption (in transit and where applicable at rest)
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication safeguards

8. Your Privacy Rights

A. GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Users)

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise rights: support@desibhasha.com

B. CCPA / CPRA Rights (California Residents)

Under California law, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Opt-out of sale of personal data

Important:

  • We DO NOT sell personal data
  • We DO NOT share children’s data for advertising

To submit a request: support@desibhasha.com

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries.

We ensure appropriate safeguards such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable

10. Third-Party Services
