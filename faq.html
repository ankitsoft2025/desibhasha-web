Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is DesiBhasha?
DesiBhasha is a specialized language-learning platform designed for Indian families living abroad. We offer interactive lessons in 10 major Indian languages—Hindi, Punjabi, Telugu, Marathi, Gujarati, Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada, Bangla, and Odiya—tailored specifically for children aged 4 and older.
2. Which age group is the app designed for?
Our primary curriculum is designed for children aged 4 and above. The interface is intuitive and child-friendly, making it easy for young learners to navigate through alphabets, numbers, and stories with minimal parental assistance.
3. What specific languages are available?
We currently support 10 languages:
  • North & West: Hindi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Marathi
  • South: Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam
  • East: Bangla, Odiya
4. How does the app teach the language?
We use a multi-sensory approach to learning. This includes:
  • Visuals: Alphabet tracing and word associations.
  • Audio: Native pronunciation for words, sentences, and poems.
  • Storytelling: Short stories and cultural narratives to build comprehension.
  • Gamification: Interactive tests and quizzes at the end of every category to track progress.
5. Does the app cover Indian culture as well?
Yes! At DesiBhasha, we believe language and culture are inseparable. Our curriculum includes sections on Indian traditions, festivals, nature, and local crafts to help children understand the context behind the words they are learning.
6. What is the "Live Translation" feature?
Our Live Translation tool allows users to input phrases in English and receive an instant, accurate translation into any of the 10 supported Indian languages. This is a great tool for children to quickly find out how to say their daily thoughts in their mother tongue.
7. Can my child learn to read and write, or just speak?
Our app covers both! We start with the fundamentals of the Alphabets (reading and tracing) and move toward building vocabulary and small sentences, ensuring a balanced development of speaking, reading, and basic writing skills.
8. Are there assessments to track my child’s progress?
Absolutely. Every category—whether it’s Numbers, Animals, or Short Stories—concludes with a fun, interactive test. This helps parents see where their child is excelling and which areas might need a little more practice.
9. Why should I choose DesiBhasha over general language apps?
Unlike general apps, DesiBhasha is built specifically for the "Desi" diaspora. We focus on the unique challenge of maintaining heritage languages while living in an English-speaking environment, using cultural nuances and content that resonates with Indian families.
10. How can I get started?
Getting started is easy! Simply download the app, select your preferred heritage language, and let your child begin their journey back to their roots.
